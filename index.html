About Me

As a UI & UX Geek, I have a passion for creating both the web presentation layer and experiences that make it useful and compelling. The two disciplines, User Interface Development and User Experience Design, are very different but very complimentary. By developing user interfaces, I engineer a user’s access to a powerful web application. By design user experiences, I optimize how that web application is used and the success of its usage.

I’ve been working with the web for the past 20 years, wearing several hats on many projects. Over that time I’ve become experienced with just about every aspect of web production. I’ve also worked with several business verticals including automotive, e-commerce, marketing, nonprofit, sports, energy, and medical.
