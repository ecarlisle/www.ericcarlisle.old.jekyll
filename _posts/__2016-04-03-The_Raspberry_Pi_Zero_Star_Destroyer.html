Let's start with one of Raspberry Pi's newest innovations, The Raspberry Pi Zero. Next we'll wrap a few hundred nanoblocks around this brilliant computer and shape it with some Star Wars fandom. Geekery ensues!

I send this out as a salute to an organization that's inspiring global educational empowerment with the creation of extremely low-cost computer hardware. Just when I think The Raspberry Pi Foundation couldn't inspire me more, they blow me away again! The Raspberry Pi Zero is a $5 computer board. With the addition of some peripherals and adapters the Zero can be made into a human-usable computer system at an ashtonishingly low cost.
