For me, client-side asset management has always a tedious process - that is to say, it's a real pain in the arse! What libraries do I need? What libaries are in use? Do I have the right versions? Why is this library covered in dust? Upon using Bower package manager for the first time, I fell in love. Suddenly my world was filled with unicorns, double rainbows and all without the need of ellicit substances. You may ask why I attribute such praise to this tool. This is because Bower makes the process of client-side asset management both managable and encouraged.
